This no-nonsnse website will allow you to track your personal fitness records (for short: PR). Choose from pre-set options for weight lifting, crossfit and running or add your own. This website is a side-project of my own that allows me to track my own PRs and will therefore always be free.

If you have an account, go directly to your dashoard. If not, register a free account to start tracking your own PRs.

Feel free to check the changelog for this website or have a look at my personal (everchanging) backlog to see which features are coming up.

If you feel like you miss any additional functionality that is not on the backlog or requires immediate attention, or you need support regarding this website, contact me through the contact form.
